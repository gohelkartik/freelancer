Platform Safeguards

What is the AI Match Finder?

It uses data-driven algorithms to rank freelancers based on skills, ratings, and previous success on similar project types.

How does the Secure Wallet work?

Clients deposit funds upfront which are held by WorkWave. This protects freelancers from non-payment and clients from incomplete work.

Is my communication private?

Yes, but our automated system monitors for abusive language or unprofessional conduct to maintain a high-quality environment.

What are Milestone Payments?

Large projects are broken into smaller tasks. Payments are released step-by-step as each phase of the project is approved.
